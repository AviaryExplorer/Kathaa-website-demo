Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence

Founded by Chef Antoine Laurent in the heart of the city, Lumière began as a humble bistro with a vision to redefine fine dining. Today, we stand as a beacon of culinary innovation, blending classical techniques with contemporary artistry.

Every dish tells a story, sourced from local artisans and sustainable farms. Our open kitchen invites you to witness the passion and precision that goes into every plate.
